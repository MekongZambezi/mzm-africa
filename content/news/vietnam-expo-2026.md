MZM Africa attended the Mining & Construction Vietnam Expo held in Hanoi in April 2026, marking the firm's formal commercial debut in the Southeast Asian investor and OEM market.

## A Strategic Debut

The expo provided MZM's Hanoi-based team with direct access to Vietnamese and regional OEM equipment manufacturers, project financiers, and commodity traders — all gathered under one roof.

Operating under what the team calls a "Two-Hat Strategy," MZM engaged with the market as both potential equipment buyers (facilitating procurement intelligence for Zimbabwe-based operations) and as selective capital-raising operators for qualifying investors.

## Chrome & Gold Opportunities Presented

MZM presented two primary investment opportunities to qualified prospects at the event:

**Chrome Wash Plant Model** — A 50 TPH alluvial chrome wash plant on Zimbabwe's Great Dyke corridor, requiring $310,000–$410,000 in capital with an estimated 2.6-month payback at full utilisation. The opportunity is underpinned by Zimbabwe's February 2026 blanket raw mineral export ban, which has created urgent demand for domestic beneficiation infrastructure.

**Gold Cluster Model** — A hub-and-spoke gold processing model targeting ASM producers across Zimbabwe's greenstone belts, using gravity separation and VAT leach processing routed through Zimbabwe's Fidelity Gold Refinery.

## What's Next

MZM is currently in follow-up dialogue with several qualified prospects introduced at the expo. Investors interested in either opportunity are encouraged to reach out via our contact page.
